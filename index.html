About Me

I'm a software engineer based in Kuala Belait, Brunei Darussalam that has some experience as a graphic designer, technical support and data analyst. Although I have these experiences, I still find myself to go back to my passion of building exceptional web experiences from small business websites to rich, interactive web applications.

Typing lines of codes, building the software, delivering the finished product, shows that programming gives value to people. It allows myself to solve your problems. Make ideas come to reality. Bring your web presence to the next level. Build the website you have always wanted. It's why I pursued and became a Computer Science graduate, and why I'm still passionate about programming.

Other than my profound interest in web development, I also have an interest in Internet-of-Things, Cyber Security and Artificial Intelligence. It's really fun to build a project where I combine all these interest of mine into one large project.

When I am not in front of the screen, I can be found reading books, playing with my cats, and playing indie games.
